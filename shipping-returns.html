Shipping & Returns

Shipping Policy

We currently ship only in Delhi and Noida. Orders are usually processed within 1–2 business days after confirmation.

Delivery typically takes 3–7 business days depending on your location and courier partner.

Once your order is shipped, you will receive tracking details via WhatsApp or email.

Delivery Charges

We offer FREE shipping on all prepaid orders.

Cash on Delivery (COD), if available, may include an additional charge.

Returns & Exchanges

We accept returns or exchanges only in case of:

  • Damaged product
  • Incorrect item delivered
  • Manufacturing defects

To be eligible, you must contact us within 48 hours of delivery.

Products must be unused, unwashed, and in original packaging.

How to Request a Return

Please contact us on WhatsApp or Instagram with:

  • Your Order ID
  • Images or video of the issue

Our team will guide you through the next steps.

Refunds

Once the return is approved and inspected, refunds (if applicable) will be processed within 5–7 business days.

Refunds are issued to the original payment method.
